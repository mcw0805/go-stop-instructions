Hwatu Deck Breakdown

A deck of hwatu is comprised of

  • Month Cards (48)
  • Jokers Cards (up to 6)


Month Cards

Month cards are a group of 4 cards, where each group corresponds to the 12 months in the Gregorian calendar (i.e. January to December). Hence, hence there are 4 x 12 = 48 cards.

Within the month group, each card associated with a rank that determine their value or worthiness.

Special Cases
  • September yeol can be used as yeol or ssangpi. Depending on the region or what the player agrees on, the player makes such choice in ONE of the following manners:
    • When they collect the card.
    • Anytime during the game.
    • When a player wins.

Joker Cards

A standard deck of hwatu usually comes with 5 to 6 jokers. The jokers mary vary depending on the brand.

Examples of joker cards:

  • Plus Pi
  • Two Pi
  • Three Pi
  • Block Bright
  • Congratulating Pi

The players may decide to not use any or use only some. As an example, many Internet Go-Stop games use two Two Pi jokers and one Three Pi.

There is also no obligation for the players to abide to the joker labels. They may decide to use the Three Pi as another Two Pi.
